# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: November 27, 2024
**Effective Date**: November 27, 2024

zk-Intents ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information**:

- Email address
- Password (stored as bcrypt hash, never plain text)
- Optional: Display name

**Authentication Data**:

- WebAuthn credentials (passkey public keys)
- Device information (for passkey management)

**Transaction Data**:

- Wallet addresses (generated from your seed phrase)
- Transaction history (intents submitted)
- Token balances

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data**:

- IP address (anonymized after 24 hours)
- Browser type and version
- Device type (mobile, desktop, tablet)
- Operating system
- Referral source
- Pages visited and time spent
- Clickstream data

**Technical Data**:

- Cookies and similar tracking technologies
- Session identifiers
- Error logs (anonymized)

### 1.3 Blockchain Data

**On-Chain Data** (publicly visible):

- Wallet addresses
- Transaction hashes
- Smart contract interactions
- Token balances

**Note**: This data is permanently recorded on public blockchains and cannot be deleted.

### 1.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

- Social Security numbers or government ID
- Full credit card numbers (we don't handle fiat)
- Seed phrases or private keys (you manage these)
- Biometric data (passkeys use device hardware, we only store public keys)
- Location data (beyond country-level for compliance)

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Technical Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption at Rest**: AES-256 for sensitive data
- **Encryption in Transit**: TLS 1.3 for all connections
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, principle of least privilege
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and alerting

### 5.2 Organizational Measures

- Employee background checks
- Security training for all staff
- Incident response plan
- Regular security audits

### 5.3 Your Responsibility

- Keep your password and seed phrase secure
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Use a trusted device for passkey creation
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### 5.4 No Guarantee

While we implement reasonable security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use the Services at your own risk.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

### 6.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of your personal data by emailing privacy@zkintents.io.

### 6.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate data through your account settings or by contacting us.

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your account. Note:

- On-chain data cannot be deleted (blockchain immutability)
- We may retain some data for legal compliance (7 years for financial records)
- Anonymized data may be retained for analytics

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can export your data in JSON format from your account settings.

### 6.5 Right to Object

You can object to processing for direct marketing at any time.

### 6.6 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we temporarily stop processing your data in certain circumstances.

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for optional features at any time through account settings.

### 6.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we have violated your privacy rights, you can file a complaint with your local data protection authority.
